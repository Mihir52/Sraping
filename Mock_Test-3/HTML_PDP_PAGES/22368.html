Recapture your handsome black hair with ICan London Triple Action Black Hair Color Shampoo no-fuss instant blackening shampoo. Turns your hair black in 5 minutes that lasts up to 4 weeks. Strengthens hair, improves silkiness and shine.

ICan London Triple Action Black Hair Color Shampoo Key Features

  • Provides long-lasting results ( up to 4 weeks)
  • Formulated with natural ingredients
  • Extremely easy to use (achieve results in just 4 steps)
  • Enriched with growth factors and antioxidants
  • Free of Sulphates, Ammonia, PPD and parabens
  • Rich in Vitamin B5 which helps restore damaged hair
  • British Brand

Active Ingredients

Radix Lindeae – darkens hair tone and provides nourishment
Ginseng – Improves shine and colour
Ganoderma Lucidum – Prevents hair from losing its natural hue and sheen and fight off premature greying
Ginkgo Biloba – A leaf extract which increases blood flow to hair follicles and reduces hair loss
Angelica Extract – Improves thickness and reduces breakage
Fleeceflower Root – Promotes blood circulation to the scalp resulting in controlling grey tones and prevents hair loss

How to use

  1. Get a skin allergy test before you use this shampoo.
  2. Wet hair thoroughly
  3. Put on plastic gloves (free with the package).
  4. Take the required amount and rub it on to your hair.
  5. Keep massaging for about 5 minutes.
  6. Leave the creamy shampoo on for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Rinse well with water.
