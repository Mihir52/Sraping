Moroccanoil Moisture Repair Shampoo gently and effectively cleanses and revives hair leaves it nourished, manageable and strong. Formulated with antioxidant rich argan oil, reconstructive keratin, lavender, rosemary, chamomile, and jojoba extracts and fatty acids, this moisture repair shampoo repairs damaged strands and dead ends to restore elasticity, moisture and luminous.

Moroccanoil Moisture Repair Shampoo Key Features

  • Color safe, sulfate, phosphate and paraben free
  • Specially formulated for damaged and weakened hair
  • Does not wash hair colour away
  • Restores moisture and reinvigorates scalp
  • Helps repair weak and damaged hair

How to use

  1. Massage Moisture Repair Shampoo throughout wet hair and scalp
  2. Add water to make a rich lather
  3. Rinse thoroughly until water runs clear
  4. Repeat if needed
  5. Follow with Moroccanoil Moisture Repair Conditioner.
