Moisturizing shampoo for dry, dehydrated and nutrient-lacking hair. Revlon Pro You Nutritive Shampoo is a moisturizing and nourishing shampoo for dry and dehydrated hair. Formulated with ceramide protein extracts. Its action closes the scales of the cuticle and recovers the hydrolipidic film that protects the hair from dehydration.

With the Revlon Nutritive Shampoo, the hair regains strength, vitality and shine. Beauty solutions adapted to each type of hair for a 100% professional result.

Revlon Pro You Nutritive Shampoo Key Features

  • Provides in-depth hydration and nourishment to dry, dehydrated hair lacking nutrients
  • Containing soy extract and linseed oil that nourishes the hair through the action of essential amino acids, vitamins and mineral
  • Regulates hydration and provide strength and shine to hair fibres

How to use

  1. Apply to wet hair starting with ends toward roots.
  2. Make a lather.
  3. Rinse well.
  4. Follow with Revlon Hydrating Nutritive Treatment.
